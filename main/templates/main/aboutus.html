About Us

What We're All About

Welcome to Lokolingo , where learning knows no boundaries and passions find their place to thrive. Were not just an online hub for education; were a vibrant community that connects students and tutors for language learning and a world of exciting extracurricular activities.

Our Story

Lokolingo was born out of a shared love for languages and a commitment to expanding horizons through extracurricular pursuits. Our journey began with a simple yet profound idea: to create a space where students can embark on linguistic adventures and discover a multitude of hobbies and interests under the guidance of passionate tutors.

Our Mission

Who We Are

Our mission is to bridge the gaps in language learning and provide a platform where students can explore diverse interests. We’re here to make learning languages like Kikuyu, Luo, Kamba, Kalenjin, and many more accessible. At the same time, we’re dedicated to nurturing talents in activities like chess, coding, playing musical instruments, and much more. Our aim is to inspire curiosity, ignite passions, and unlock the potential in students one language and one passion at a time.

Join Us

Whether you’re passionate about learning new languages, eager to excel in chess, or want to unlock the secrets of coding, Lokolingo welcomes you with open arms. Education is not just about mastering subjects; it’s about exploring the world of possibilities. Thank you for choosing Lokolingo. Let’s embark on this journey of learning, discovery, and growth together. Join us and let your language skills and passions flourish!

Global Community
