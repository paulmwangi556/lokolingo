About Us

Welcome to Lokolingo , where learning knows no boundaries and passions find their place to thrive. We’re not just an online hub for education; we’re a vibrant community that connects students and tutors for language learning and a world of exciting extracurricular activities.

Our Story

Lokolingo was born out of a shared love for languages and a commitment to expanding horizons through extracurricular pursuits.

Our journey began with a simple yet profound idea: to create a space where students can embark on linguistic adventures and discover a multitude of hobbies and interests under the guidance of passionate tutors.

Our Mission

Who We Are

Our mission is to bridge the gaps in language learning and provide a platform where students can explore diverse interests. We’re here to make learning languages like Kikuyu, Luo, Kamba, Kalenjin, and many more accessible. At the same time, we’re dedicated to nurturing talents in activities like chess, coding, playing musical instruments, and much more.

Our aim is to inspire curiosity, ignite passions, and unlock the potential in students’ one language and one passion at a time.
